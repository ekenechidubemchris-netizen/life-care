Our Story

Why we opened our doors

LifeCare began as a single consulting room and grew because the community around it needed somewhere reliable — somewhere a test result wouldn't get lost, a prescription wouldn't get rewritten three times, and a patient's history would actually be on hand at the second visit.

Today that same commitment shapes everything we do, from how we register a new patient to how we track medicine stock in our pharmacy.

MISSION
Our Mission

Accessible, well-documented, dignified healthcare for every patient, however complex or routine their need.

VISION
Our Vision

A hospital where no file is ever lost, no prescription is ever miscommunicated, and every staff member owns their part of the journey.

VALUES
Our Values

Respect for every patient's time and dignity, accuracy in every record, honesty in every conversation about cost and care.

Legally Recognised

Our incorporation and licensing

CAC
Corporate Affairs Commission

LifeCare General Hospital Ltd. is duly incorporated under Nigerian law, giving it the legal standing to own property, employ staff, and be held accountable as a registered business.

MDCN
Medical & Dental Council of Nigeria

Every doctor at LifeCare holds a valid licence to practice issued by the MDCN, confirming they've met the council's training, examination, and ethical standards.

STATE HEALTH AUTHORITY
Health Facility Monitoring & Accreditation Agency

The facility itself is registered with, and periodically inspected by, the State Ministry of Health, reviewing our equipment, hygiene standards, staffing, and record-keeping.
